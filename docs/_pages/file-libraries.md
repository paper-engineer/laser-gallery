A NOTE ABOUT COMMERCIAL LICENSING

table these. note license terms are a broad approximation. also some of these are just vector art sites. 

## Entire laser files for cutting

[design bundles](https://designbundles.net/free-design-resources/free-laser-cutting-files): 
Commercial licensing for physical products. Redistribution of files not allowed, remixing to upload allowed, POD may be allowed. More designs locked behind paid membership, with a free trial for 7 days or 30 products (whichever you hit first). 

[3axis](https://3axis.co/free-svg-files/):
Commercial licensing for physical products. Redistribution of files not allowed, remixing to upload NOT allowed, POD not allowed. 

### aggregator sites

No license stated, might be per file, idk, requires free login, I didn't bother: [ponoko](https://www.ponoko.com/free-laser-cutting-files-templates)

License per file: [atomm](https://www.atomm.com/laser-projects) [vecty](https://vecty.co/) [omtech1](https://omtech.com/collections/diy-craft-kits) [omtech2](https://ideas.omtech.com/) [obrary](https://obrary.com/collections/designs-for-the-laser) [cults3d](https://cults3d.com/en/laser-cutting-cnc-machining)

### not precisely laser aggregator sites, but has laser files

License per file: [thingiverse](https://www.thingiverse.com/) [makerworld](https://makerworld.com/en)

Non-commercial only: [grabcad](https://grabcad.com/library)

## Just vector designs

[freesvg](https://freesvg.org/): public domain

[freevector](https://www.freevector.us/free-svg-files/): No license stated. Use at own risk. 

[vecteezy](https://www.vecteezy.com/free-svg/laser-cut): Complex licensing that pretty much boils down to non-commercial and requires attributing. 

[magnific](https://www.magnific.com/vectors/laser-engraver-files-svg): Complex licensing that pretty much boils down to non-commercial. Includes AI-generated files.
