A request for a renfaire booth sign. This was a large area fill of 3mm ply. 

Material: 3mm pine ply

Time: about an hour figuring out settings, and 40 minutes to engrave

Yeah so I ran a couple of grids and complained in chat about the engrave time - at 30x30cm, the whole thing would have taken about an hour and some change to engrave. Chat was very helpful and suggested defocusing and increasing the line spacing, so I did that. Of course this comes with increased power requirement, but we were already looking at 20%, it's not like the laser couldn't handle it? 

Settings: defocused it with laser head 1.7cm above the material surface. engrave at 12k/30, line interval 0.15mm (up from standard 0.1mm). still cuts at 450/100, even, or 400/90. 

I also tested on the back of the ply, the side with the nice lines. Flipping it over gives the engrave some very obvious texture, which is cool. Also changes the engraving settings: 9k/40 seems to be the right answer there. No difference to cut settings, but I didn't think there would be.
