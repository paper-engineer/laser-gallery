Material: 3mm pine ply, not much to it

Time: about an hour figuring out settings, and 40 minutes to engrave

Interesting settings note

large area fill of 3mm ply
defocused it to 1.7cm above the material surface
12k/30, line interval 0.15mm (up from standard 0.1mm)
still cuts at 450/100, even, or 400/90. 

now testing on the back of the ply, with the nice lines
flipping it over gives the engrave some very obvious texture, which is cool
but then 9k/40 instead
